Baldwin Public Library has three study rooms for use by Baldwin Public Library cardholders. These rooms include seating for four, a large table to work on, and a monitor upon which to cast your screen.

To use one of these rooms, please check in at the Adult Services reference desk with your library card. These rooms are checked out on a first come, first served basis. You may use the rooms for up to two hours at a time.
